Sierra was born in Arizona but has lived most of her life in southern Nevada. She graduated from high school early and earned an Associates of Arts degree in Creative Writing from the College of Southern Nevada. After serving a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints to southeastern Idaho, she fell in love with the area. She returned to Rexburg to continue her schooling. She is currently working to complete a degree in Interdisciplinary Studies with an emphasis in Creative Writing and Computer Information Technology. In her spare time, Sierra enjoys playing computer games, reading, and spending time with her friends and family.

A Word from the Author

Ever since I was a little girl, I was fascinated with the creation of stories. Everything to me was an epic adventure that I was a part of. Once I learned how to read for entertainment, my whole world opened up in a way I never thought possible. New adventures became available to me. I already had an active imagination as a child and would always tell stories to my family. Once I figured out that normal people like me wrote books, I knew that one day I would share my own written stories with the world. From that moment on, I wanted to be an author. The earliest story I remember trying to write down was about two girls who ran away into a magical forest. Though I never completed that story, it formed the base for what is now the Reaching Yana series.
