Welcome to Rynum

Rynum is a magical planet outside of the solar system we have known all our lives. In fact, it is part of its very own solar system in the very center of a nebula (which acts as a barrier to keep in all magic particles). While unknown to Earthians, the people of Rynum have studied their own solar system. Rynum is the second planet from the sun and not the only one that is inhabitable. Rynum's sister planet has nearly the same atmosphere as Rynum, but it has yet to be colonized due to there being an over-abundance of carnivorous plants. The other planet that holds life is Eebor, the gas giant similar to our own Jupiter. A small colony has sprung up there underneath all the gases where there is a breathable atmosphere (granted, there are a few side effects from all the other gases).

Rynum itself contains unique characteristics. No where else can there be found elves and dwarves, dragons and mages, fairies and humans all coexisting as in many famous Earthian stories. Portals are another facet unique to Rynum. Though originally the portals did not exist, they dot the land (even some parts of the sea) and connect Rynum to Earth.
