Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide readers with a site they can go to in order to purchase books and gain more information about the world I create.

Audience

The audience will be those who read my books. I will write mainly for young adults, but anyone who likes or loves to read (or who just happens to pick up my books out of curiosity) is more than welcome.

Typography

Heading Fonts: Cinzel and Eagle Lake

I chose Cinzel as a heading font because it provides a sense of structure and formality. Though not necessary, I chose to include a second heading font to more smoothly transition from my heading fonts to my paragraph font. While Cinzel provides the formality needed, Eagle Lake provides a sense of fantasy where there would otherwise be none. This is important to me because I want visitors to my site to not only see the structure but also to get a feeling for the novels I write, which are part of the fantasy genre.

Paragraph Font: Baloo Bhaina 2

I chose Baloo Bhaina 2 as my paragraph font because I felt it went well with both my heading fonts. It also is fairly easy to read and has a softness that neither of the heading fonts contain.
